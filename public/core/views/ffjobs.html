Jobs
Jobs or in other games known as classes are the decider in your charachters fighting style and how you can affect the battlefield. Each job brings it's own unique abilities, traits and role.
Tanks
Able to hold an enemy's attention tanks are the first in and last out. Every tank has the ability to mitigate damage and hold aggro providing a secure hold for the party.
