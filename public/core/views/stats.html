Stats and Materia

Basic Stats

HP

HP stands for Hit Points or Health Points. When you take damage it will reduce your HP. If your HP reaches zero your charachter will die, being unable to perform any actions.

MP

MP stands for Mana Points or Mana Pool. Mana is used for most magic actions ex: Cure, Fire, ect. If you do not have sufficient Mana to cast your spell you won't be able to start casting.

TP

TP stands for Techinical Points. TP is used by mainly classes who use weapons for damage and is used upon execution of a weapon skill.

Block

Parry

Physical Defense

Reduces damage taken from incoming Physical Attacks

Magic Defense

Reduces damage taken from incoming Magical Attacks

Attack Power

Attack Power increases damage from outgoing Physical Attacks

Attack Magic Potency

Increases damage dealt from outgoing Magical Attacks

Healing Magic Potency

Increases healing done from outgoing Healing Spells (not all healing actions scale off Healing Magic Potency)

Primary Stats

Strength

Strength is the primary damage dealing stat for all tanks and melee jobs except Ninja. Strength has a 1 to 1 weight meaning 1 strength equals 1 attack power. Strength also increases the amount of damage you mitigate when you block or parry an enemy attack.

Dexterity

Dexterity is the primary damage dealing stat for Bard, Ninja, and Machinist. Dexterity also increases the rate at which you block and parry. Dexterity has a 1 to 1 weight to attack power

Vitality

Vitality directly increases your HP.

Intelligence

Intelligence is the primary offensive stat for Caster DPS. Intelligence directly affects Magic Attack Potency for these Classes

Mind

Mind is the primary stat for healers. For healers Mind directly effects Attack Magic Potency and Healing Magic Potency.

Secondary Stats

Critical Hit

Critical Hit Rate increases the chance of an attack dealing critical damage and increases the damage of a critical hit.

Determination

Determination increases the amount of damage dealt by all attacks auto-attacks, weaponskills, spells, ect. It also increases the amount of HP recovered by spells.

Direct Hit

Direct Hit increases chance of landing a "Direct Hit", think of it as a mini critical hit

Tenacity

Tenacity is on tank gear only and it increases damage dealt, reduces damage taken, and HP restored. Currently it seems that for every 200 tenactiy is a 1% increase for the effects.

Skill Speed

Skill Speed reduces the cast time and cooldown of weaponskills. It also increases Physical DoT's (Damage over Time) damage per tick.

Spell Speed

Spell Speed reduces the cast time and cooldown of spells. It also increases Magical DoT and HoT's damage/healing per tick.

Piety

Piety Directly increases MP
